RelX Coffee Policy

General Conduct

  • All customers are expected to treat staff, other patrons, and pets with respect and courtesy.
  • No disruptive behavior, including loud noises or inappropriate language, is permitted.
  • Personal belongings and pet accessories should not obstruct walkways or seating areas.
  • RelX Coffee is a smoke-free and vape-free environment.

Pet Policy

  • Well-behaved pets are welcome in designated outdoor seating areas.
  • Pets must be leashed or in a carrier at all times.
  • Owners are responsible for cleaning up after their pets, including any waste or spills.
  • Pets must not disturb other customers or their dining experience.
  • Only service animals are permitted in indoor seating areas, in compliance with local regulations.
  • RelX Coffee reserves the right to ask owners to remove disruptive or aggressive pets.

Payment Policy

  • We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments (e.g., Apple Pay, Google Pay).
  • All prices are inclusive of applicable taxes unless otherwise stated.
  • Payment must be completed before receiving food or beverages.
  • Tips are appreciated but not mandatory.

Refund and Cancellation Policy

  • Refunds are issued only for incorrect or defective orders.
  • Please inform staff immediately if there is an issue with your order.
  • No refunds for consumed items or change of mind.
  • For catering or bulk orders, cancellations must be made 24 hours in advance for a full refund.

Health and Safety

  • Customers with allergies must inform staff before ordering.
  • No outside food or beverages are allowed, except for personal water bottles and pet water bowls provided by RelX Coffee.
  • Children and pets must be supervised by an adult at all times.
  • RelX Coffee follows all local health regulations to ensure a clean and safe environment for all patrons and pets.
